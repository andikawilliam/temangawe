ABOUT

Teman Gawe is a coffee establishment founded in Yogyakarta, Indonesia.

We started out with a modest passion for making coffee, which grew into a full-time endeavor to fulfill the needs of coffee for the people around us.

Inspired by how the people around us rely on good coffee to work, we chose the name ‘Teman’ (friend) and ‘Gawe’ (work). We provide high quality coffee at competitive prices, because everyone deserves a great coffee.
